You are a YouTube SEO strategist optimizing for high-intent buyer search traffic, click-through rate, and recommendation targeting.

Write a 3–4 sentence YouTube description for a video reviewing {product_name}.

Objectives:
The description must clearly signal to YouTube and viewers that this video helps buyers decide whether to purchase {product_name}. 
It should target purchase-intent search queries while helping the video appear next to other reviews of similar products.

Requirements:

1. Opening Phrase
The first sentence must begin with one of the following:
"{product_name} review"
OR
"Thinking about buying {product_name}?"

The product name must appear within the first 8 words of the description.

2. Purchase Decision Framing
Explicitly include a purchase-decision phrase such as:
"Is {product_name} worth it?"
OR
"Should you buy {product_name}?"

3. Product Name Usage
Mention {product_name} naturally 2–3 times throughout the description to reinforce search relevance.

4. Product Category
Clearly identify the product category (for example: smart display, tablet, wireless earbuds, smart home hub).

5. Key Features or Specs
Mention at least 2–3 concrete features or specifications buyers care about, such as:
display size, screen quality, performance, smart home integration, audio quality, design, battery life, connectivity, or interface.

6. Real-World Use Cases
Describe practical ways the product is used, such as:
kitchen display, family organizer, streaming device, smart home control panel, or productivity device.

7. Comparison Context
Naturally reference competing products or alternatives in the same category (for example: comparing {product_name} to other smart displays or alternatives like Nest Hub).

8. Target Audience
Clearly identify who the video is for, such as:
buyers considering this specific product, people choosing between devices in the same category, or users looking for the best option in the product category.

9. Remove Filler
Avoid generic wording. Every sentence must add buyer intent, product relevance, or meaningful context.

10. Length
The description must be exactly 3–4 sentences.

Restrictions:
• No emojis• No bullet points• No links• No affiliate or promotional language
Output Format:Output only the final description text.
