Create a high click-through-rate YouTube thumbnail for a tech review video.

CORE PRIORITY:
• The product must be the largest object in the frame and visually dominant.
• The viewer’s eye should go to the product first, then the headline text, then the human face.
• No fictional elements.
• Do not include unrelated objects, metaphors, or symbolic elements.
• Only include the real product and realistic human interaction.

MANDATORY COMPOSITION:
• The large product must strongly dominate the LEFT side of the frame (approximately 50% or more of visual weight).
• The product should sit naturally on a desk or table with realistic perspective.
• The product must appear sharp, highly detailed, and clearly lit.
• Subtle rim lighting or soft highlight may be used to separate it from the background, but keep it realistic.
• Do not center the product.
• Do not redesign, exaggerate, or stylize the product.

HUMAN ELEMENT:
• Include a real-looking tech reviewer on the RIGHT side.
• The face must support the product, not compete with it.
• Natural skin texture, realistic lighting, slight asymmetry.
• Subtle, believable expression that matches the headline tone.
• No exaggerated shock faces.
• The person should be interacting naturally with the product (looking at it, adjusting it, pointing, reacting).
• The face must be smaller in visual weight than the product.

BACKGROUND:
• Realistic blurred tech environment.
• Natural depth-of-field.
• Slight background darkening to increase product contrast.
• No dramatic cinematic effects.
• No glowing fantasy lighting.

TEXT:
Headline: "{headline_text}"

• Large, bold, thick sans-serif text.
• High contrast against background.
• Strong drop shadow.
• Clear outline stroke around letters.
• Slight separation glow behind text for readability.
• Text should pop clearly at small mobile sizes.
• 2–4 words maximum.
• No clutter or extra phrases.

STYLE:
• Clean, high-end YouTube tech review aesthetic.
• Balanced lighting between product and face.
• Professional but realistic.
• Should look like a thumbnail designed by an experienced human creator.
• Avoid over-processing or poster-like AI styling.

GOAL:
The thumbnail must feel real, product-focused, trustworthy, and opinion-driven.
The product should clearly be the hero of the frame.
The human supports the message.
The text reinforces the opinion.
