Create a high click-through-rate YouTube thumbnail for a tech review video.

CORE PRIORITY:
• The small product must be the largest and most visually dominant object.
• The viewer’s eye must go to the product first, then the headline text.
• The product must visually outweigh the hands.
• The product must clearly separate from the background.
• No fictional elements.
• Do not include unrelated objects, metaphors, or symbolic elements.
• Only include the real product and realistic human hands.

MANDATORY COMPOSITION:
• This layout is ONLY for small products.
• The product must be held naturally in one or two hands.
• The product must occupy the LEFT side of the frame.
• The product must be larger in visual weight than the hands.
• Do not center the product.
• Do not redesign or stylize the product.

PRODUCT VISUAL IMPACT (VERY IMPORTANT):
• The product must be the brightest and most contrast-rich object in the frame.
• Increase sharpness and clarity on the product only.
• Add subtle rim lighting or edge lighting around the product to separate it from the background.
• Slight background blur increase behind the product.
• Slight background darkening behind the product.
• Ensure strong contrast between product edges and background.
• The product must not blend into similar-colored background areas.
• If background is dark, ensure product highlights are brighter.
• If product is dark, ensure background behind it is lighter.
• Clear edge definition around the product silhouette.

HUMAN REALISM:
• Only hands visible — no face.
• Natural skin texture with subtle imperfections.
• Realistic shadows between fingers.
• Casual grip — not posed.
• Balanced lighting consistent with the environment.
• Hands must not visually overpower the product.

BACKGROUND:
• Realistic blurred home desk or tech setup.
• Natural depth-of-field.
• Stronger blur directly behind product.
• Slight vignette to subtly focus attention toward product.
• No neon glow or cinematic fantasy lighting.

TEXT:
Headline: "{headline_text}"

• Large, bold, thick sans-serif font.
• High contrast color.
• Strong outline stroke.
• Strong drop shadow.
• Clear separation from background.
• 2–4 words maximum.
• Instantly readable at mobile size.

STYLE:
• Clean, authentic YouTube tech review aesthetic.
• Product-first lighting hierarchy.
• Realistic shadows and depth.
• Slight natural imperfections allowed.
• Avoid over-processed AI poster styling.

GOAL:
The product must immediately stand out and feel like the focal point.
It must not visually merge with the background.
The hands add realism and scale without overpowering it.
The text reinforces a strong opinion clearly.
